About Us

Welcome to the Student Council of Prince Mohammed bin Fahd University (PMU)! At PMU, the Student Council represents the voice of the student body, dedicated to enhancing student life and fostering an inclusive, engaging campus environment. We work closely with students and the university administration to ensure that student concerns, ideas, and initiatives are heard and addressed, empowering every student to make the most of their university experience.

Our Vision

We envision a university where every student feels empowered to contribute to their community. Through our efforts, we aim to inspire a sense of belonging and responsibility, fostering a dynamic campus culture that reflects the diversity and aspirations of all PMU students.
